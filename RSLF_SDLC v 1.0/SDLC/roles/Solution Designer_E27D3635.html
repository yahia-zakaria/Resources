    Main Description

    Solution designer review and consult architecture team during building the SAD document and choosing the technology stack.

    The solution designer has main responsibility to guide, audit and collaborate with delivery/development teams to build and maintain the solution components and artifacts and to guide the design and implementation decisions.

    Solution designer activities may include the following:

    ·       Consult and Review Architecture Team’s artifacts (SAD documents)

    ·       Create and Maintain Solution and Application Low Level Design (answering the question of How) including all technical aspects and identifying the right technologies and practices that suites the business requirements and the proposed solution

    ·       Design, Develop and Maintain Platform Frameworks (Cross-Cutting, Non-Functional)

    ·       Create and Maintain development best practices and manifests

    ·       Review, Validate and Approve (from technical perspective) the delivery artifacts including the solution and application components

    ·       Mentorship of development, Guide and Support the Delivery/Development teams during the project lifecycle
    Staffing
    Skills

    ·       Deep Software Engineering skills (OOP, FP, SOLID, Design-Patterns, Integration-Patterns …etc.)

    ·       Deep analytical and problem-solving skills

    ·       Deep technical and development skills

    ·       Deep SDLC knowledge

    ·       Good communication, soft, and technical writing skills

    ·       Deep data and databases development, tuning and enhancement skills (RDMS and NoSQL)

    ·       Deep IT architecture, infrastructure, networks, mobile, integration and cloud development skills

    ·       Good DevOps understanding and skills
