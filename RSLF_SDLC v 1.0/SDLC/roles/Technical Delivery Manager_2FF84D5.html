    Key Considerations

    §  Responsibilities:

    1.     Develop the section’s (Technical Delivery) vision, and mission statements.

    2.     Establish long-term objectives of the section.

    3.     Generate, evaluate, and select strategies for the section.

    4.     Develop annual objectives for the section, and the Technical Delivery Leads.

    5.     Manage and oversee the Technical Delivery Leads.

    6.     Assign a Technical Delivery Lead for current or new projects.

    7.     Develop, maintain processes related to Technical Delivery.

    8.     Communicate with section’s internal and external stakeholders regarding Technical Delivery related topics.

    9.     Hire, train, develop, and evaluate Technical Delivery Leads.

    10.  Monitor the projects led by the Technical Delivery Leads, and provide the needed support, e.g., escalation, request needed resources, etc.
