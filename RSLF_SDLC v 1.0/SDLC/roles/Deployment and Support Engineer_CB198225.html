Role: Deployment and Support Engineer
This role leads planning the builds transition across Environments and product's transition to the user community, ensuring those plans are enacted appropriately, managing issues and monitoring progress.

    Skills

    A person playing the Deployment Engineer role requires the following skills:

     

    -  Experience in deploying systems. 

    -  Communication/Coordination in order to stay current with the status of the product development and communicate the needs of the deployment tasks to the rest of the organization. 

    -  Planning Ability in order to ensure that deployment can be performed on schedule and with the available resources. 

    -  Goal-orientation and Pro-activity in order to plan and drive the product to completion across the various teams. The Deployment Manager role has to focus on getting a quality product out the door.
    Assignment Approaches

    To be effective, the Deployment Engineer and the Project Manager roles must work closely together. As such, these roles are often realized by a single person.
