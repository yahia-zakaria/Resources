Role: Test Manager (QC)
Managing and overseeing the function’s activities, through developing and updating a convenient testing framework entailing all guidelines and rules used for creating and designing test cases, developing and updating test automation frameworks, reviewing all set plans and schedules for the testing activities, initiating and leading any enhancement plans while keeping abreast of modern and advanced testing techniques and tools and following up on their implementation.

    Key Considerations

    Strategic Duties

     

    1.

     

    Developing the Section’s work plans and specifying all necessary activities and tasks to serve the achievement of the Department’s/ Sector’ objectives.

     

    2.

     

    Supervising the implementation of the Section’s plans, monitoring workflow, and ensuring adherence to the set policies and procedures.

     

    3.

     

    Ensuring that work activities are carried out according to the preset performance objectives and suggesting any necessary actions to the Department’s Head.

     

    4.

     

    Participating in setting the Department’s budget, in addition to monitoring and controlling the expenditures in coordination with the Finance Sector.

     

    Technical and Executive Duties

     

    1.

     

    Developing and updating a convenient testing framework entailing all guidelines and rules used for creating and designing test cases.

     

    2.

     

    Developing and updating test automation frameworks, including coding standards, test-data handling, object repository treatment, etc. which resulting in beneficial outcomes like increase code re-usage, higher portability, reduced script maintenance cost, etc.

     

    3.

     

    Reviewing all set plans and schedules for the testing activities, including manual, automated and performance testing to ensure their effectiveness and providing recommendations accordingly.

     

    4.

     

    Resolving any raised complex issues related to manual, automated and/or performance testing, conducting root cause analysis on such to prevent recurrence.

     

    5.

     

    Ensuring proper coordination with other functions within TETCO in order to facilitate the process of fixing the detected bugs, glitches and other flaws in a timely manner.

     

    6.

     

    Initiating and leading any enhancement plans in the function, including introducing new testing tools, methodologies, etc. whilst ensuring the adherence to the frameworks and guidelines developed by the Center of Excellence function.

     

     

    7.

     

    Keeping abreast of modern and advanced testing techniques and tools, providing recommendations in this regard, and following up on their implementation.

     

    8.

     

    Maintaining a defect triage process and continuous defect review for the developed solutions to ensure proper evaluation, prioritizing and assignment of defects’ resolution.

     

    9.

     

    Reviewing the implemented quality metrics, such as defect densities, open defect counts, etc. spotting any trends or patterns and taking the proper action accordingly in alignment with TETCO’s policies and procedures.

     

     10.

     

    Performing any other duties related to the job as assigned by the direct supervisor.

     

    Administrative Duties

     

    1.

     

    Conducting periodic meetings with subordinates to ensure that priorities are clear and workflow is running smoothly.

     

    2.

     

    Preparing periodic reports with respect to the Section’s activities and achievements.

     

    3.

     

    Ensuring that instructions, circulars and organizational and operational decisions are followed and applied.

     

    4.

     

    Ensuring Section’s workforce requirements are met according to specified workforce plans.

     

    5.

     

    Appraising subordinates’ performance according to scheduled plans, and recommending necessary actions based on the evaluation results as per the adopted practices.

     

    6.

     

    Recommending training needs of subordinates and evaluating the outcomes of training courses in coordination with the concerned Department.

     

    7.

     

    Following up on subordinates’ administrative affairs, such as vacations, leaves, departures, etc.

     

    8.

     

    Nominating a deputy to carry out the responsibilities of the position whenever the need arises.
