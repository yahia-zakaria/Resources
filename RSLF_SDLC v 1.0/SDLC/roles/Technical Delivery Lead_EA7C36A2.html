    Key Considerations

    §  Responsibilities:

    1.     Act as a Developer to finish development tasks related to assigned projects, systems, or products.

    2.     Act as a Technical Lead to lead and drive all technical aspects of a project, a system, or a product. For example, technical project management, build, test, go-live, stress test, penetration test, and post go-live support.

    3.     Act as a Scrum Master to facilitate, and help everyone understand Scrum theory and practice, both within the Scrum Team and the organization. Scrum Master may help also in removing impediments that face the team, ensuring that all Scrum events take place, and helping in refining the product backlog with the Product Owner.

    4.     Act as a Level 3 Support to fix incidents that could not be fixed by Level 2 Support nor Level 1 Support.

    5.     Oversee the technical-related activities of the project.

    6.     Ensure that expectations are aligned with regards to the goals, scope, and timelines for product delivery.

    7.     Facilitate on project, product, or system releases level planning across multiple teams.

    8.     Guide, coach, manage, and assign the team on delivering solutions that fit our client’s requirements as well as plan.

    9.     Coordinate with project managers, product managers to develop timelines, raise risks.

    10.  Understand the skills, needs, responsibilities of the team members working on the project, and suggest or facilitate the needed trainings.

    11.  For outsourced project, this will be based on the contract details, however, it is preferable to engage in all technical-related activities.

    12.  Possess comprehensive knowledge about the led projects.

    13.  Communicate with the team, management, internal stakeholders, and external stakeholders regarding any technical-related matters.

    14.  Review/approve different kind of project deliverables, e.g., documentation, source code, running application, test report, test cases, deployment plan, performance test, etc.

    15.  Help infrastructure team in provisioning testing, staging and production environments.

    16.  Actively track metrics reported by the related Scrum Masters.

    17.  Advise and guide teams to understand the best delivery approach that fit the project, the product, or the system e.g., Waterfall, Scrum, Kanban.

    18.  Cooperate closely with colleagues in onshore and offshore locations across the globe.

    19.  Ensure that all risks and issues are appropriately managed in alignment to the defined (Agile, Waterfall) delivery approach.
