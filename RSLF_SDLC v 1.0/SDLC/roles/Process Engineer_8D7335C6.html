Role: Process Engineer
This role is a support role in the PIM. Its main goal is to equip the project team with an appropriate development process, and to make sure that the team members are not hindered in doing their jobs.
Relationships

    Main Description

    A person acting as process engineer must be a good facilitator and have excellent communication skills. Knowledge of the software development business domain is essential for those acting in this role; however, it is not necessary for other roles. The responsibilities of the Process Engineer can be detailed as follows:

     

    1.       Establishing and maintaining the PIM organization’s set of standard processes.

    2.      Establish and maintaining tailoring criteria and guidelines for the Organization’s set of standard processes.

    3.      Establishing and maintaining the Oganization’s measurement repository.

    4.      Establishing and maintaining the Organization’s process asset library (PAL).

    5.      Establishing and maintaining the different Osoftware development work environment standards.

    6.      Establishing and maintaining the organizational rules and guidelines for the structure, formation, and operation as well as vendor teams.

    8.      Establishing and maintaining the description of process needs and objectives for the organization development work environments.

    9.      Appraising the organization’s processes periodically and as needed to maintain an understanding of their strengths and weaknesses.

    10.   Identifying improvements to the Organization’s processes and process assets.

    11.    Establishing and maintaining process action plans to address improvements to the Organization’s processes and process assets.

    12.    Deploying the Organization’s set of standard processes to software projects at their startup and deploys changes to them as appropriate throughout the life of each project.

    13.   Monitoring the implementation of the Organization’s set of standard processes and use of process assets on all projects.
