Task: Sprint Review
A sprint review is demonstrating the hard work of the entire team: designers, developers, and the product owner. At Atlassian we like to keep sprint reviews casual. Team members gather around a desk for informal demos and describe the work they’ve done for that iteration. It’s a time to ask questions, try new features, and give feedback. Sharing in success is an important part of building an agile team.
