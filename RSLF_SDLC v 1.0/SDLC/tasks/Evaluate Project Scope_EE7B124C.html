Task: Evaluate Project Scope
This task defines how to develop a Business Case by identifying the fundamental Product, Business, and Estimation aspects of developing a new product, as well as the major constraints and options to consider.
Disciplines: Project Management
Relationships
RolesPrimary Performer:
  • Project Manager (PM)

    Outputs
    • Business Case
    • Tailoring Request
    • Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)
      Main Description
      Steps
      Step 1. Study Customer Requirements and Technical Approach with the Project Team
      Step 2. Identify Project Deliverables and Key Work Products
      Step 3. Estimate Size and Complexity of the Project
      Step 4. Prepare the Business Case
      Step 5. Publish the Business Case for Review
      Step 6. Obtain Project Participants and Team Members’ Commitments (by email or meeting)
