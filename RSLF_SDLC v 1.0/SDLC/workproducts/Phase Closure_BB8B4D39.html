Artifact: Phase Closure
This artifact is created to capture officially an end of phase (milestone) review results which in turn the phase or the milestone is formally accpeted and closed.
Work Product Kinds: Project Management
Purpose

The purpose of this artifact is to formally the status and results of end of phase review in order to officially accept and close the milestone. This artifact uses more than one template based on the milestone type.
