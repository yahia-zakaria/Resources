This artifact involves the evaluating performed processes, work products, and services against the applicable process descriptions, standards, and procedures and identifying and documenting noncompliance issues.

 

This artifact is attached to the Reviews Work Item to ensure providing feedback to project staff and managers on the results of quality assurance activities as well as ensuring that noncompliance issues are addressed
