Artifact: Project Workspaces
This work product is a set of tools that enable MeduNet team to collaborate and coordinate their efforts on building the software product and completing the project.
Work Product Kinds: Project Management
Purpose

The NPIM  is used with a variety of tools-from Microsoft Team Foundation Server and SharePoint which are designed to enhance MeduNet  development team communication, track work status, support team roles, enact the team process, and integrates team tools. The project workspaces are including:

1- Team Foundation Team Project

2- Team Foundation Source Control

3- Team Project SharePoint Portal
