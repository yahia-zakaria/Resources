Work Product Descriptor (Artifact): Business Case
The purpose of the Business Case is to evaluate and appraise the project size and characteristics to give a solid basis for later detailed planning. Its goal to develop an economic plan for realizing the project vision presented in the Work Product: Vision.

Main Description

Once the Vision is developed; the Business Case is used to make an accurate assessment of the effort and schedule consumed by the project. This Work Product is mandatory; to ensure that project scope and risks are managed effectively.
