Task Descriptor: Close-Out Project
This task describes how to complete the formalities associated with project acceptance and close-out, reassign project staff, and transfer other project resources.

Main Description

“Close-Out Project” occurs after the final delivery of software or all change requests. The expectation is that there remain no problems that will preclude formal acceptance, and that any issues that do remain are documented and handed over for resolution to the customer or other maintenance organization.

Steps
Step 1. Collect Feedback from System Users in Production Environment
Step 2. Release Project Resources
Step 3. Perform Quality Audit on Quality Assurance Audit
Step 4. Close Project Workspaces and Schedule
Step 5. Ensuring that the Project is Formally Accepted
Step 6. Record Decisions and Review Results
Step 7. Ensuring that Organization measures are updated from Project Manager
Step 8. Close-Out the Project by sending email to PM, PSM, Software Engineer Manager, Process Engineer, QA, CM to notify them about closing the project formally.
