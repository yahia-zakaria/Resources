Task Descriptor: Sprint Retrospective
Sprint retrospectives happen after the Sprint Review and before the next Sprint Planning. They help a Scrum team review its process and identify opportunities to improve it. Sprint retrospectives are considered a continuous improvement opportunity for a Scrum team to review the good, the badâ€”and the ugly
During the Sprint Retrospective, your team should discuss four main questions:

What did you like in the sprint?
What's still puzzling you?
What didn't work well?
What are your improvement ideas?
