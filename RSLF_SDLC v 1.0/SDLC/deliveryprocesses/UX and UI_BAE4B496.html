Main Description

This role is primarily responsible for negotiating the ongoing purpose and deliverables of the Graphic Design effort, ensuring the appropriate planning and management of the Graphic Design resources and assessing the progress and effectiveness of the raphical Design effort. The responsibilities of the Graphic Design Team Leader can be detailed as follows:

 

1.       Distribute Graphic Design  tasks to Graphical Design and monitor the work effort .

2.      Establishing and maintaining the environment needed to support the Graphic Design  process.

3.     Establishing and maintaining procedures and criteria for Graphic Design  in respect to the standard Naseej Graphic Design  policy.

4.    Analyzing results of Graphic Design activities against the defined testing criteria.

5.    Allocating and transferring resources across product groups and projects.

6.   Resolving issues affecting the Graphic Design progress.

7.   Analyzing defect seepage to production and ensures that actions are taken for enhancing Graphic Design effectiveness
