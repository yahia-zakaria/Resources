Description

Summary

 

The Envision phase is of significance primarily for new development efforts (e.g. vendor related projects), in which there are significant business and requirements which must be addressed before the project can proceed.

 

Objectives

 

Objective 1               

Establishing the project's software scope and boundary conditions, including an operational vision, acceptance criteria and what is intended to be in the product and what is not.

 

Objective 2              

Discriminating and detailing the use cases of the system, the primary scenarios of operation that will drive the major design tradeoffs.

 

Objective 3             

Refining potential risks that have been identified earlier in the Initiating Stage in the lifecycle.

 

Objective 4              

Preparing the supporting environment for the project.

 

Objective 5             

Identify the project organizational plans (i.e. Software Development Plan) needed to develop the software application effectively and efficiently during the project. This objective is essential for all vendor related projects in the NIC.

 

Milestone

 

At the end of the Envision phase; the first major project milestone or Lifecycle Objectives Milestone. At this point, Project team examines the objectives of the project up to specific evaluation criteria like;

- Do all stakeholder concurrence on scope definition?

- Are requirements have been captured and that there is a shared understanding of them?

- Do all stakeholder concurrence on the software development plan and releases?
